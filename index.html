How It Works

Setup in minutes.
Secured for decades.

Getting started with Bedrock is straightforward. True self-custody was never meant to be complicated.

01

Unbox & Power On

Bedrock ships sealed in a tamper-evident package. Power on with the physical button — no software required to begin.

02

Generate Your Keys

The device generates a cryptographically random 24-word seed offline. Write it down on the steel backup card provided.

03

Receive Bitcoin

Scan the QR code displayed on-device to get your receive address. Send Bitcoin from any exchange or wallet.

04

Sign Offline

When spending, scan the unsigned transaction QR into Bedrock, sign it offline, then broadcast the signed QR from any device.
